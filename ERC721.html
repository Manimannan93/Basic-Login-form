ERC-721

explanation:

The ERC-721 standard is a token standard on the Ethereum blockchain designed for creating non-fungible tokens (NFTs). Unlike ERC-20 tokens, each ERC-721 token is unique and indivisible, representing a specific asset or item. ERC-721 tokens have gained immense popularity for their ability to tokenize and represent digital art, collectibles, gaming assets, virtual real estate, and other unique assets on the blockchain.
